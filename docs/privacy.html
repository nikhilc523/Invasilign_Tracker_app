Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2025

Overview

InviSmile ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

The short version: We don't collect, store, or share any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Information We Do Not Collect

InviSmile does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Health or medical data
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Cookies or tracking technologies

Data Storage

All data you enter into InviSmile — including treatment plans, wear time records, tray information, and settings — is stored exclusively on your device and in your personal iCloud account (if iCloud sync is enabled).

We have no access to your iCloud data. Apple's iCloud infrastructure handles all synchronization, and the data is encrypted end-to-end.

iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync, your data will be stored in your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit framework. This allows your data to sync across your Apple devices. This feature is optional and can be disabled at any time in the app's settings.

Your iCloud data is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy and is protected by Apple's security measures.

Third-Party Services

InviSmile does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not share any data with third parties.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, they are generated and scheduled entirely on your device. No notification data is sent to or processed by any external server.

Children's Privacy

InviSmile does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not require any personal information to function.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
