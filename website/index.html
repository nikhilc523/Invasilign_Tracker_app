Frequently Asked Questions

How does wear time tracking work?

InviSmile calculates your daily wear time by subtracting the time your aligners are out (removal sessions) from 24 hours. Simply tap the timer when you remove your aligners and tap again when you put them back in.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. All data is stored locally on your device and synced privately via your personal iCloud account. We have zero access to your data and don't collect any analytics or personal information.

Does it work with Apple Watch?

Yes! InviSmile includes a full Apple Watch companion app with complications, allowing you to track sessions and view progress directly from your wrist.

What are the activity rings?

Inspired by Apple Fitness, our three rings represent: Wear (daily wear time goal), Streak (consecutive days meeting your goal), and Life (overall treatment compliance). Fill all three to stay on track!

Can I change my wear time goal?

Yes, go to Settings and adjust your daily wear target between 18-24 hours. You can also configure grace minutes and compliance mode to match your orthodontist's recommendations.

How do I switch to a new tray?

Navigate to the Trays tab and tap the add button to register your new tray. The app will automatically track your progress for each tray based on the days-per-tray setting.
